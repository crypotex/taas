  • Paying by MakeCommerce
  • Paying by Pro Forma (prepayment) invoice
    • {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} NB! Paying by prepayments budget and by internet bank is possible only by logging in with an activated account. Read here how to make an account.

Paying by prepayments budget
After you have marked the desired dates in the calendar on your (activated) account, pressed “Submit”, agreed to the general and purchasing terms and chosen payment method “Budget” then you must enter your password to finalize the deal, which decreases your account´s budget and confirms the made reservations. To add money to your account press the tab “Balance”, enter the sum you wish to add and press “Submit” to pay by internet bank.

Paying by MakeCommerce
After you have marked the desired dates in the calendar on your (activated) account, pressed “Submit”, agreed to the general and purchasing terms and chosen payment method “Transaction”, you must choose the bank from which you wish to make the transfer from. Then you will be redirected by the Payment Center to the corresponding internet bank, where you can finalize the payment to make the reservation or to increase your budget.

NB! Payments done by MakeCommerce will be settled in Euros and the sum from your account will also be deducted in Euros!

By Makecommerce you can use the payment links from the following banks: {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %} It is also possible to receive card payments from Visa, MasterCard & Maestro.

Paying by Pro Forma (prepayment) invoice
Paying by Pro Forma invoice is possible only for corporate bodies, who wish to provide reservations for certain periods throughout the whole season. Outer season lasts from the 1st of April until the 30th of September and inner season from 1st of October until 31st of March.

Pro Forma invoice should be requested by sending the reservation wishes for the whole season to agility.arenguselts@gmail.com. The Pro Forma invoice will be sent to you during the next 3 business days, which you can then pay by internet bank or in bank's offices. Reservations cannot be added before the whole (100%) of the Pro Forma invoice´s sum is received by Tartu Agility Arenguselts. If the Pro Forma invoice is not paid in 14 days after issuance, then the reservation wish will be cancelled. {% endblocktrans %}
