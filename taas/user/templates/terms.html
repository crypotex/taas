Terms and Conditions of Purchase along with guidance to make the reservations and manage the account

In order to make a reservation choose a suitable date from the calendar and then select one or more available time slots in the right table column (depending on which field(s) you want). The start time of the slot you choose has to be at least 30 minutes from now. To deselect a slot click on it once more. It is possible to book fields for multiple dates, just choose another date from the calendar.
When you have finished choosing, click "Next" to proceed to the payment page. Review your selections and if needed you can remove time slots that you do not need. You can pay using your account balance money (pre-added money) or pay through a bank link.
You have 10 minutes to complete your reservation. When time is up, all your selections will be removed.
There are 3 different fields available for booking. You can see a description of each field by hovering over the corresponding letter in the table header.
There are 4 colours to distinguish between different types of reservations. Hover over a colour below to see the description.
Click on the "History" tab, you should see the table with all of your reservations. Reservations are in descending order, i.e. upcoming reservations are on the top while older ones are below them. You can sort the table by field, start or end time. To see reservations from the past or next month, select the month above the table and click "Sort".
You can change time or/and field of your reservation whenever you want but not 15 minutes before the start of the reservation. That means if you have, for example, a reservation starting at 14:00, then you can make changes until 13:45. Note that you can only choose a new time or/and field for the same date as the reservation that you are trying to edit. You can remove a reservation 24 hours before the day of the reservation. E.g. you have a reservation on 29th of November, then you can cancel it until 23:59 on 28th November. The money will be added to your account's balance.

In order to modify an upcoming reservation click on the "History" tab, you should see the table with all of your reservations. If you can edit a reservation (check Is it possible to modify an upcoming reservation?), then there will be a pencil icon next to the reservation, click on it. You should see an alert asking for confirmation. If you really want to change the reservation then click "OK", otherwise decline by clicking "Cancel". You should see a table where a red slot indicates the reservation you are trying to change. Drag it to another slot that suits you and is free. Then click "Submit".

In order to cancel an upcoming reservation click on the "History" tab, you should see the table with all of your reservations. If you can remove a reservation (check Is it possible to cancel an upcoming reservation?), then there will be a cross icon next to the reservation, click on it. You should see an alert asking for confirmation. If you really want to delete the reservation then click "OK", otherwise decline by clicking "Cancel".

In order to see and change the account information click on the "Account" tab, you should see the account modification page. You can update your name, phone number and password. If you want to change your password, then click on the checkbox and fill in all the fields, otherwise leave checkbox unchecked and just click "Update" to finish. You cannot leave name and phone number fields empty. You cannot change your e-mail for security reasons. If you really need to change your e-mail, then contact the administrator.

In order to add money to the account balance click on the "Balance" tab, you should see the account balance page. Fill in the amount you wish to add to your account balance and click "Submit" to proceed to pay.
